what is sport

An activity involving physical exertion and skill in which an individual or team competes against another
or others for entertainment. "team sports such as soccer and rugby"
synonyms: (competitive) game(s), physical recreation,
physical activity, physical exercise; pastime "he takes part in a variety of sports"
